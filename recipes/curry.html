Curry

Description

A classic butter chicken recipe that is easy to make and everyone will love. Just make sure you have a few (hundred) calories to spare today!

Ingredients

Spices/Aeromatics

  • cumin
  • black pepper corns
  • tumeric
  • chili powder
  • bay leaf
  • salt

Main

  • high quality canned tomatoes
  • honey
  • butter
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • shallot
  • jalapeno
  • coconut cream

Steps

  1. sautee shallot, garlic and butter until blackened.
  2. toss in cubed chicken thighs.
  3. blend tomatoes, ginger, garlic, jalapeno, cumin, black pepper corns, bay leaf.
  4. while chicken is still raw pour mixture over.
  5. add tumeric, chili powder, salt, honey, butter, coconut cream (all to taste!)
  6. Once chicken is cooked, you are done. Be sure not to overcook it!
